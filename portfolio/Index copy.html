    Freight and Freight Movement are clear indicators of economic vitality. By understanding the connections of freight movement throughout the United States we can see connections in the economic system of the country.

    These connections also may allow policy makers the opportunity to focus on the primary freight routes for various purposes. Freight routes can be the focus of investment creating jobs needed for maintaining these routes that get so much wear. Safty issuse could also be addressed, as primary freight routes are often highly congested increasing the probability of accidents.
